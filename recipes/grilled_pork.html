Samgyupsal Gui (Grilled Pork Belly)

Simple, easy Korean BBQ with unseasoned fresh prok belly.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sliced fresh pork belly (Sampgyupsal)
  • 8 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 4 green chili peppers, roughly sliced
  • 3-4 scallilns, thinly sliced (or pa muchim)
  • 2 to 4 ounces mushroom caps- white, crimini, or king mushrooms- optional
  • 4 chili peppers -optional
  • 2 heads of red or green lettuce
  • 16 perrilla leaves,(kkaennip)- optional

Sesame oil sauce(per person)

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • black pepper to tase

Ssamjang (for wraps)- serves 4

  • 2 tablespoon doenjang(Korean soybean paste)
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean red chili pepper paste)
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine or mirin -optional

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables. Mix the sauce ingredients for each sauce.
  2. Heat the grill(or a flat pan or grill pan).Cut the prok belly strips in shorter bite size pieces. You can also grill first and cut into bite sized pieces with kitchen scissors.
  3. Add the meat to the grill along with the garlic, chili peppers, and any other begetables your are using. Grill over moderate heat until cooked throughm flipping once. It's best to serve right off the grill or pan as you cook.
